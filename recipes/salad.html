Chicken Salad

The classic Chicken Salad, made with roasted chicken, lettuce, and your choice of fruits and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Lettuce
  • Assorted Fruits & vegetables

Steps

  1. Grill chicken.
  2. Place lettuce on plate or in bowl.
  3. Place cooked chicken and chosen fruits & veggies on lettuce.
