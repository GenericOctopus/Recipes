Pizza Rolls

Delicious bite-sized pizzas.

Ingredients

  • Bag of Pizza Rolls
  • Oven or Air Fryer

Steps

  1. Place pizza rolls on baking pan or in fryer basket.
  2. If using oven, heat your rolls at 425 degrees F for 10 minutes; if using air fryer, cook at 400 degrees for 5 minutes.
  3. Allow your rolls to cool, then enjoy!
