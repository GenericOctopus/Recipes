Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

The classic PB&J sandwich, made with peanut butter, choice of bread, and grape jelly.

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Place 2 slices of bread on plate.
  2. Spread peanut butter over one of your slices of bread.
  3. Spread jelly over the remaining slice of bread.
  4. Press the slices of bread together so that the peanut butter and jelly meet.
